# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our community a welcoming, inclusive, and respectful experience for everyone, and to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, diverse, and healthy community.

## Standards

We expect participants to:

* Demonstrate empathy and kindness toward other people
* Respect differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
* Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
* Take responsibility, apologize when mistakes affect others, and learn from the experience
* Focus on what is best for the overall community

Conduct that is unwelcome includes harassment, personal attacks, publishing others' private information without permission, and other behavior that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces, and also when an individual is officially representing the community in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and will take appropriate corrective action in response to any behavior that they deem inappropriate, threatening, or harmful.

Reports may be sent to **gina@cleancoders.com**. All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly. Reporters' privacy will be respected.

Enforcement consequences follow the [Contributor Covenant Enforcement Guidelines](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/#enforcement-guidelines), ranging from a private written notice up to a permanent ban for sustained or severe violations.
